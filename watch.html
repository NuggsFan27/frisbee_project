How to Throw a Backhand

The video below demonstrates the backhand throw. This is the easiest and most common throw you will use in the game. As the video demonstrates, the most important component of this throw is your hip/foot placement and the amount of 'snap' you can generate with your wrist for distance. The video is produced by Rowan McDonnell; he is a professional ultimate player famous in the D.C. Metropolitan area.
